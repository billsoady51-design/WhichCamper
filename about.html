Our story

Built by someone who got completely lost trying to buy a van

WhichCamper exists because buying a campervan or motorhome in the UK is genuinely confusing - and there was nothing out there that gave honest, specific advice without trying to sell you something.

It started with a simple question: VW T6 or Mercedes Sprinter? What followed was months of YouTube rabbit holes, forum arguments, contradictory advice from dealers and a growing sense that nobody was giving straight answers.

The van conversion world in particular is a minefield. Dozens of converters, hundreds of base vehicles, wildly different quality levels and almost no independent guidance on what actually matters - width for country lanes, weight for your licence, payload for your kit, gearbox for your driving style.

So we built the tool we wished had existed. WhichCamper asks the questions that actually matter - not just "what's your budget" but what roads you drive, whether you need to stand up inside, how long you can go off-grid, whether you care about DSG or not - and matches you to a specific base vehicle, a specific converter brand and a specific model.

It covers both campervan conversions and coachbuilt motorhomes. It tells you what compromises you are making. It shows you what it will cost to run. And it sends you directly to the right place to buy.

No signup. No spam. No affiliate pressure to recommend one brand over another. Just honest matching.
